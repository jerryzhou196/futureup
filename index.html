Who are we?

FutureUp is an organization for driven young entrepreneurs or future business leaders to meet, network, and learn from other youth or industry professionals in the community! We're an organization for youth, run by youth. Get involved in your future and stay tuned for more!

Think you have a million dollar idea? Here are some local resources to help you get started.

League Of Innovator's Connector Role

Becoming a LOI Connector means getting your hands dirty: diving deep into the entrepreneurship world, meeting innovators in your city and connecting them with each other. As a Connector, your main goal is to build a community of current and aspiring young entrepreneurs and remove obstacles for youth to participate in entrepreneurship. Connectors will be supported centrally, and are encouraged to self direct and create new opportunities to create, inspire and foster innovation and entrepreneurship in their regions.

Apply Here!
Platform Calgary Summer Incubator Program

The Summer Incubator is a 4-month work integrated learning program that provides student entrepreneurs with everything they need to launch and grow a startup! This program offers post-secondary students a unique opportunity to spend their summer receiving valuable support including access to Advisors and formal programming throughout the summer (May-August). At the end of the program, students will demonstrate what they have learned and how it’s changed their business, traction they’ve achieved, and what their next steps will be.

More Info!
Platform Calgary Junction Program

Ever wish there was a playbook to help grow your startup? Junction is a 9-week residency program that helps startup entrepreneurs, like you, establish clear foundations for technology-driven businesses. Throughout the program, you will learn to develop and organize information that allows you to understand and articulate your company to anyone, clearly. As the program progresses, you will actively build a data room - an organized compilation of startup documents in a secure location - which acts as a backdrop to educate, inform, and understand all aspects of a startup business.

More Info!
League Of Innovator's Foundation Program

Ready to build your entrepreneurial dreams with the League of Innovators? Sign up now for our Foundations program and take the first steps to build your startup in our 8-week acceleration program. There are approximately 75 minutes of programming delivered online through video conferences each week. The next batch of the program will run from March 17th to May 4th. The program features webinars, coaching, online tools and content to help you start to make your dreams a reality. Let's go from idea to your MVP (minimum viable product)!

Junior Achivement Company Program

Over a four-month period, JA’s Company Program teaches grade 9-12 students how to organize and operate a real business. Volunteers from the local business community work with students to launch and run a small enterprise. This gives students the real-world skills and experience that they need to achieve their dreams of becoming entrepreneurs.

League Of Innovators

The League of Innovators (LOI) is a national Canadian charity with a goal of building entrepreneurial acumen for youth, at scale. We have a suite of accessible programs and tools to provide youth educational experiences that shorten their path to success, empower their ability to be their own boss, and amplify their impact as future innovators and entrepreneurs.

Royal Bank Of Canada

The Royal Bank of Canada (RBC; French: Banque Royale du Canada) is a Canadian multinational financial services company and the largest bank in Canada by market capitalization. The bank serves over 16 million clients and has 80,000 employees worldwide. The bank was founded in 1864 in Halifax, Nova Scotia, although its corporate headquarters is located in Toronto, Ontario, and its legal head office in Montreal, Quebec. RBC's institution number (or bank number) is 003. In November 2017, RBC was added to the Financial Stability Board's list of global systemically important banks.
